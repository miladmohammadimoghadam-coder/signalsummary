Privacy Policy

Audio: When you record, your voice is sent to your browser's built-in speech recognition (a free Google service) to turn it into text. SignalSummary itself does not store, save, or listen to your recordings — nothing is kept on any server.

Account info: If you sign up or sign in, we store only your mobile number, verified by an SMS code, so you can access your account. We never sell or share this with third parties.

Local preferences: Your light/dark mode choice is saved only in your own browser, to remember your preference next time.

No ads, no tracking: This site doesn't run ad networks or third-party trackers.

Questions about this policy? Email signalsummarysupport@gmail.com.

Terms of Service

Using the service: SignalSummary lets you record or upload audio to get a transcript and an AI-generated summary. The free plan includes a limited number of short test recordings per device. By using the site, you agree to these terms.

Your content: You're responsible for what you record or upload. Don't use the service for anything illegal, or to process audio you don't have the right to use.

Accuracy: Transcripts and summaries are generated automatically and may contain mistakes. Don't rely on them for anything where accuracy is critical (legal, medical, or financial decisions) without double-checking against the original audio.

No guarantees: The service is provided "as is," built and maintained by one independent developer. Features, limits, and pricing may change as the project develops, and the service may occasionally be unavailable.

Accounts: If you sign up, keep your login secure. You're responsible for activity on your account.

Questions about these terms? Email signalsummarysupport@gmail.com.

Frequently asked questions

Is my audio saved anywhere? ▾
No. Your recording is processed to generate the text, then deleted. We don't keep a copy.
What languages are supported? ▾
English and Persian right now. More languages are planned for a future update.
Is it really free? ▾
Yes, the core features are free to use. Sign up only if you want to save your history.
What file formats can I upload? ▾
MP3, WAV, and M4A are supported, up to 25MB per file.
